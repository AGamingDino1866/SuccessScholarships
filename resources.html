Student resources

Prepare a stronger scholarship application.

Use this page before opening the form. It gives students simple guidance for documents, essays, university research, and final review.

Documents

What to prepare

  • Student full name and current class
  • School name and city
  • Guardian contact details
  • Accurate email for updates
  • Short financial need explanation

Essay help

Tell your story

Write clearly about your education, goals, family situation, and why support would help you continue learning.

Review

Before submitting

Check spellings, phone numbers, email address, class, city, and every required answer before sending the form.

Application tips

Small things that matter.

  • Use the same email everywhere so your application is easy to find.
  • Keep answers honest, specific, and simple.
  • Explain need without exaggeration.
  • Ask a parent or teacher to review your details.
  • Submit once your information is complete.

Prep tracker

Mark your progress.

  • I checked the eligibility page.
  • I prepared my student and school details.
  • I wrote my financial need explanation.
  • I reviewed my contact information.
  • I am ready to open the application form.

University research prompts

Think beyond the form.

Academic goal

What subject do you want to study, and why does it matter to you?

Dream university

Which university inspires you, and what program would you want there?

Community impact

How would continuing your education help your family or community?

Next milestone

What is one realistic step you can take this month toward your education?
