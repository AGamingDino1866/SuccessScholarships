Can I apply?

How we check need.

The team looks at money, family situation, school costs, and effort in school. Income is important, but it is not the only thing.

40%

Monthly family income

This shows how much support the family may need for daily life and school costs.

15%

Family size

A bigger family may need more support, so family size is checked too.

15%

School costs

Fees, exams, transport, books, uniforms, internet, and other school costs may be checked.

15%

Family hardship

Extra care may be given for orphan students, single-parent homes, disability, illness, displacement, or disaster loss.

10%

Guardian work

Daily wage, temporary, seasonal, or no work can show unstable income.

5%

Study effort

Students should show attendance, effort, and a good school recommendation.

Income guide

Monthly family income guide

  • Below PKR 45,000: most priority
  • PKR 45,001-60,000: high priority
  • PKR 60,001-80,000: some priority
  • PKR 80,001-100,000: small chance
  • Above PKR 100,000: usually not eligible unless there is serious hardship

Documents

Documents you may need

After you apply, email the documents that apply to you as PDF files to successscholarships2026@gmail.com. Put your application ID in the email subject so the team can match the files to your application.

  • CNIC or B-Form and Family Registration Certificate
  • Income certificate, salary slip, and utility bill
  • Admission letter, fee challan, and attendance record
  • Teacher recommendation letter
  • Disability certificate, death certificate, or BISP/Zakat registration if needed
