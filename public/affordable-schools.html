Free Education

Government Schools

Pakistan offers free primary, middle, and high school education through government institutions.

How to Get Into Government School

1. Check Age & EligibilityMust match class requirement and age group.
2. Gather DocumentsBirth certificate, father's CNIC, vaccination record.
3. Visit School (June-July)Go during admission season to school office.
4. Fill Form & SubmitFree admission form with all documents.
5. Pass Entrance TestUsually basic test in Urdu, English, Math (if required).
6. Pay Minimal FeeTypically 0-500 PKR annual fee.

Private School Admission

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Research & ContactVisit school website, call admissions office, request prospectus.
2. Take Entrance ExamUsually test in Urdu, English, Math. Interview with principal/counselor.
3. Submit DocumentsBirth certificate, school records, transfer certificate, CNIC copies, vaccination record, photos (4x4).
4. Pay RegistrationDeposit + registration fee (1,500-5,000 PKR).
5. Monthly TuitionPay fees starting next month as per school schedule.
6. Receive ID & HandbookStudent ID card and school handbook issued.

Scholarships

Financial Aid Available

Government Scholarships

5,000-15,000 PKR/year (merit + need-based)

Apply at: Higher Education Commission (HEC) - www.hec.gov.pk

Private School Scholarships

25-100% fee waivers for merit students (80%+ marks)

Schools offering scholarships: Beacon House, LGS, Aitchison, St. Patrick's

Zakat & Charity

Most schools have scholarship funds through zakat and charity programs.

How to apply: Ask at the admissions office during enrollment.

Pro Tips

Tips to Save on School Fees

  • ✓ Apply to government schools first (completely free)
  • ✓ Look for schools with active scholarship programs
  • ✓ Apply early (June-July) for better selection
  • ✓ Prepare well for entrance exams to qualify for merit scholarships
  • ✓ Ask about partial fee waivers or need-based assistance
  • ✓ Check if your parent/guardian works for the school (employee discount)
  • ✓ Ask about sibling discounts (2nd child, 3rd child get reduced fees)
  • ✓ Consider online/distance learning programs (very affordable)
