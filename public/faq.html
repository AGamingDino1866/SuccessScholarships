Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about the Sahulat Family scholarship.

Application & Eligibility

Who can apply for the Sahulat Family scholarship? ▼

Pakistani students with financial need can apply. You must:

  • Be in school, college, or university
  • Have demonstrated financial hardship
  • Be ready to commit to your education
  • Have valid documents (CNIC/B-Form, school admission letter, income certificate)
What documents do I need to apply? ▼

Prepare these documents before applying:

  • Your CNIC or B-Form (birth certificate)
  • School or college admission letter
  • Income certificate (from local government office)
  • Guardian's CNIC (parent or guardian)
  • Recent school transcript or grades

If you don't have all documents when applying, you can submit them later.

How much does the scholarship cover? ▼

The scholarship amount varies based on:

  • Your financial need (family income)
  • Your grades and academic performance
  • Available funding from sponsors
  • Type of school (government vs. private)

Scholarships typically cover partial to full tuition fees. You'll be informed of the exact amount after approval.

How long does it take to get a decision? ▼

The review process typically takes 2-4 weeks, including:

  • 1 week: Initial application review
  • 1 week: Document verification
  • 1-2 weeks: Sponsor matching

You can check your status anytime using your Application ID on the Status page. We'll also email you with updates.

Application Form & Submission

What should I write in "Why do you need support?" ▼

Be honest and specific. Explain:

  • Your family's financial situation (income, expenses)
  • Why school fees are difficult for your family
  • What education means to you
  • How the scholarship would help you continue studying

Example: "My father is a laborer earning PKR 20,000/month. My school fees are PKR 8,000/month, leaving little for food and transport. This scholarship would let me stay in school and graduate."

Tip: Use Ask AI to get help writing this section better.

What should I write in "Your study goals?" ▼

Share your dreams and future plans:

  • What do you want to study or become?
  • Why is this important to you?
  • How will education help your family?
  • What challenges will you overcome?

Example: "I want to become an engineer. My family has never had someone go to university. I will work hard to graduate and get a job that supports them."

Can I save my form and finish it later? ▼

Yes! Your form automatically saves as you type. If you close the browser and come back:

  • Sign in with the same Google account
  • Go to Apply page
  • Your saved form data will appear

Note: Saved forms are stored in your browser. Use the same computer and browser to see your saved data.

Can I submit more than one application? ▼

You can submit a new application, but:

  • The system will warn you that you already submitted
  • Your old application will be replaced
  • Only your latest application will be reviewed

If you need to update your application, use the same account and submit a new form with corrected information.

Application Status & Communication

How do I check my application status? ▼

Use the Status page:

  • Go to the Status page
  • Enter your Application ID (starts with SF2026 or SC2026)
  • See real-time updates
  • You can print or save your status

You should have received your Application ID in the confirmation email after applying.

I lost my Application ID. How do I get it back? ▼

You have two options:

  • Check your email: Search for "Application Received" or "Sahulat Family" in your email inbox (check spam folder too)
  • Use the Status page: Go to Status, enter a wrong ID, and use the "Send me my Application ID" form to resend it to your email
  • Contact us: Email sahulatfamilypk@gmail.com with your full name and the email you used to apply
Will you email me with updates about my application? ▼

Yes! We'll email you:

  • Confirmation email: Right after you submit (with your Application ID)
  • Status updates: When we review your application or need more documents
  • Final decision: Whether approved, rejected, or pending
  • Auto-reply: Immediate auto-reply if you email us with questions

Check your email regularly, including spam folder. Make sure we have the correct email address when you apply.

Ask AI & Support

How do I use Ask AI? ▼

Ask AI can help with your application:

  • Sign in to your account
  • Go to Ask AI page
  • Type your question (e.g., "How do I write my need statement?")
  • AI will suggest answers to help you

You get 150 free messages per day. AI can help with form writing, eligibility questions, and general advice.

How do I contact support? ▼

You can reach us through:

  • Email: sahulatfamilypk@gmail.com (we reply within 24 hours)
  • Contact form: Go to Contact Us page and send a message
  • Status page: Use the contact link on your status result

When emailing, include your Application ID to help us assist you faster.

Is my information private and safe? ▼

Yes. We protect your privacy:

  • Your data is stored securely in Firestore
  • Ask AI chats stay in your browser (we don't save them)
  • We never share your info with third parties
  • You can contact us to delete your data anytime

See our Privacy Policy for more details.
