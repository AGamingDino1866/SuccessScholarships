Can I apply?

How we choose students.

We look at money, family size, school costs, and school work. Money is important, but we also look at other things.

40%

Family money

How much money your family makes each month.

15%

Family size

More family members can mean more support needed.

15%

School costs

Fees, tests, travel, books, clothes, internet costs.

15%

Family hardship

Extra help if you lost a parent, have no money, are sick, can't leave, or lost things in a disaster.

10%

Parent's work

Unstable job, daily wages, or no job.

5%

School work

Show you go to class and do your schoolwork well.

Income levels

Family money levels

  • Under PKR 45,000: best chance
  • PKR 45,001-60,000: good chance
  • PKR 60,001-80,000: some chance
  • PKR 80,001-100,000: maybe
  • Over PKR 100,000: usually no, unless big problems

Papers needed

What to email us

After you apply, email your papers as PDF files to successscholarships2026@gmail.com. Put your ID in the email subject line so we can find your application.

  • CNIC or B-Form and Family Registration Certificate
  • Income certificate, salary slip, and utility bill
  • Admission letter, fee challan, and attendance record
  • Teacher recommendation letter
  • Disability certificate, death certificate, or BISP/Zakat registration if needed
Email PDFs after applying
