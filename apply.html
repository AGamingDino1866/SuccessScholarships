FAQ

Quick answers.

Do students need to create an account?

Students sign in with Google so the application can be connected to their email address.

Where do I apply?

Apply on this My Applications page using the built-in application form.

Where do I check application status?

Use the Status page and enter the application ID shown after submission.

What if I forgot my application ID?

Email the team using the same Google account email you used when applying, and include your full name and city.

Can I submit more than one application?

Students should submit once with accurate details. If something is wrong, contact the team instead of creating duplicate applications.

Can I edit my application after submitting?

Direct editing is not available yet. Contact the scholarship team with your application ID and the correction needed.

Who can see my application?

The review team can view submissions through the hidden admin dashboard. Public visitors cannot browse applications.

What statuses can my application have?

Status options include Received, Under Review, Needs Info, Approved, and Rejected.

What does Needs Info mean?

The team needs extra details before continuing the review. Check your email or contact the team with your application ID.

Do I need documents before applying?

Prepare your school name, current grade, guardian contact details, city, and a clear explanation of financial need.

What cities are supported?

The portal highlights Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, with an Other option for students outside those cities.

Can parents or guardians submit for a student?

Yes, but the student name and school details should be accurate, and the Google account email should be reachable.

What if Google sign-in uses the wrong account?

Open the Sign In page, use Sign Out, then choose the correct Google account when signing in again.

How will I know if my status changes?

Use the Status page with your application ID. The admin dashboard updates the same status record.
