FAQ

Quick answers.

Can I submit more than once?

No. The form is set up for one response per email, so use the correct email and review before submitting.

How do I check my application status?

Email successscholarships2026@gmail.com using the same email address you submitted in the form.

Which email should I use?

Use an email you can access regularly. The review team may contact you there for updates or missing information.

What happens after I submit?

Your response goes into the private review sheet. Admins review submissions and contact selected students or students who need to provide more details.

Can I edit my response later?

Assume the answer is no. Check everything carefully before submitting the final form.
