FAQ

Quick answers.

Do students need to create an account?

No. Account creation has been removed. Applications happen through the official Google Form.

Can I submit more than once?

The form is set up for one response per email, so students should review everything before submitting.

Where do I check application status?

Status updates will come through the email or contact details submitted in the form.

What if I made a mistake?

Contact the team with the same email you used in the application and explain the correction clearly.

Leaving the website

Open the official Google Form?

The form opens in a new tab. Submit only when your details are accurate because it is one form per email.
